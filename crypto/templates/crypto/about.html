How to use SEMES

  • Register as a user by filling your details to register form and generate your keys in the process
  • To generate keys, click on generate keys button and copy public key to register form and keep private key safe
  • Submit your register details
  • Log in to the system
  • You will have access to compose message and encrypt it
  • To decrypt your messages send by your friends, go to inbox page and use your private key to decrypt it to plain text
